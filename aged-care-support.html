Aged Care Support

At New Life Adventure Disability (NLA), we provide compassionate support for older participants who require additional assistance with daily living, health monitoring and social engagement.

Our aged care services focus on maintaining independence while ensuring safety, dignity and comfort for every individual.

Daily Living Support

🧑‍⚕️

Personal Care

Support with personal hygiene, grooming and mobility assistance.

🏠

Household Support

Help with household tasks, cleaning and maintaining a safe home environment.

🍲

Meal Preparation

Assistance with preparing nutritious meals and supporting healthy eating habits.

💊

Health Monitoring

Medication reminders and basic health monitoring in collaboration with healthcare professionals.

Our team works closely with healthcare providers and family members to ensure participants receive the appropriate level of care and ongoing support.

Social Engagement Programs

👥

Social Gatherings

Group programs and activities that encourage interaction and friendship.

🚶

Community Outings

Visits to parks, community centres and local events.

🎯

Recreational Activities

Fun and engaging activities designed to support mental and emotional wellbeing.

🤝

Community Partnerships

Programs delivered in collaboration with community organisations and senior centres.

Supporting Independence and Wellbeing

Staying socially connected and maintaining daily routines are essential for overall wellbeing as we age.

At NLA, we create supportive environments where older participants can remain active, connected and confident in their everyday lives.

Benefits of Our Aged Care Support

💙

Dignity & Respect

Every participant is supported with compassion, respect and personalised care.

🛡

Safety

Support services help maintain safe living environments.

🌟

Independence

Participants are encouraged to remain active and independent.

😊

Social Connection

Opportunities to connect with others and reduce isolation.

Looking for Aged Care Support?

Our team at New Life Adventure Disability is here to help older participants live comfortably, safely and independently.
