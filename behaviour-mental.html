Behavioural & Mental Health Support

At New Life Adventure Disability (NLA), we understand that emotional wellbeing is an essential part of living a fulfilling and independent life.

Our behavioural and mental health supports are designed to help participants develop coping strategies, build resilience and improve overall wellbeing through compassionate, coordinated care.

We work closely with licensed therapists, psychologists and mental health organisations to ensure participants receive professional support tailored to their individual needs.

Counselling & Therapy Support

🧠

Professional Therapy

Participants may be referred to qualified psychologists or counsellors for professional therapy sessions.

💬

Individual or Group Sessions

Support may include one-on-one counselling or group therapy depending on the participant’s needs.

🌱

Emotional Wellbeing

Therapy sessions help participants develop coping strategies and improve emotional health.

These supports help participants:

  • Develop practical coping strategies
  • Improve emotional wellbeing
  • Manage stress and anxiety
  • Build confidence and resilience

Behaviour Support Plans

📋

Structured Behaviour Plans

Through partnerships with behavioural specialists, NLA helps develop structured behaviour support plans.

✅

Positive Behaviour Strategies

Plans focus on positive behaviour support techniques designed to improve wellbeing.

👨‍👩‍👧

Support for Families

Guidance is provided for families and caregivers to ensure consistency in support strategies.

📊

Ongoing Monitoring

Behaviour plans are regularly reviewed and monitored to ensure they remain effective.

Our Collaborative Approach

At NLA, we believe that effective behavioural and mental health support requires collaboration between participants, families and professionals.

Our team works closely with therapists and mental health providers to ensure support plans are implemented consistently and effectively, helping participants achieve positive long-term outcomes.

Why Mental Health Support Matters

💪

Confidence

Participants gain confidence to navigate daily challenges.

🧩

Stability

Support plans help promote emotional stability and wellbeing.

🤝

Stronger Relationships

Participants develop better communication and relationship skills.

🌟

Positive Outcomes

Participants experience improved quality of life and independence.
