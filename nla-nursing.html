Nursing and Personal Care

Personalised nursing and daily care that promotes health, independence, and quality of life.

Get Started Contact Us

Intro

At New Life Adventure Disability (NLA), we understand that quality care means supporting both health needs and everyday living with dignity and respect.

Our nursing and personal care services are designed to help participants maintain their wellbeing while promoting independence and comfort in their daily lives.

Our team includes qualified nurses and trained support workers who provide personalised care tailored to each participant’s needs.

Nursing Services

Complex Medical Care

Professional care including wound management, catheter care and other complex medical support.

Medication Monitoring

Support with medication management and monitoring to ensure safe and effective treatment.

Healthcare Collaboration

Working closely with doctors, allied health professionals and healthcare providers.

Preventative Health Support

Education and guidance to promote long-term wellbeing and healthy living.

Where specialised care is required, NLA coordinates with local healthcare providers and medical professionals to ensure participants receive the best possible support.

Home & Personal Care Support

Personal Hygiene & Grooming

Support with bathing, grooming and personal hygiene while respecting independence and dignity.

Dressing & Mobility

Assistance with dressing and safe mobility around the home.

Meal Preparation

Healthy meal preparation and nutritional support tailored to individual needs.

Household Support

Light domestic tasks and assistance with maintaining a comfortable home environment.

Daily Wellbeing

Support with routines and activities that promote independence and personal wellbeing.

Appointments & Errands

Assistance attending appointments and completing essential errands.

Where appropriate, we collaborate with dietitians and health professionals to ensure dietary, cultural and medical needs are respected.
