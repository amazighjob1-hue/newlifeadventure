We Value Your Feedback

Your feedback helps us improve our services and better support our community.

Your Voice Matters

Feedback & Complaints

At New Life Adventure Disability (NLA), your voice matters. We welcome compliments, suggestions, enquiries and complaints. Your feedback helps us strengthen the quality, safety and impact of the supports we provide.

What Can You Provide Feedback About?

Our Services

Quality, delivery, reliability and overall experience.

Our Staff

Professionalism, respect, communication and conduct.

Communication

Information provided, clarity, responsiveness and accessibility.

Anything Else

Any experience, idea or concern you would like to share.

How to Leave Feedback

Use the form below to submit a compliment, suggestion, concern or complaint. All feedback is handled confidentially and respectfully.

You may choose to remain anonymous.

Share Your Feedback

We are committed to listening, responding fairly and improving continuously.

Your information will be handled in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

What Happens Next

01

Acknowledgement

We acknowledge your feedback promptly.

02

Review

All concerns are reviewed fairly and impartially.

03

Response

We aim to respond within 10 business days.

We aim to address any concerns as quickly and fairly as possible. NLA commits to acknowledging your feedback promptly and responding to complaints within 10 business days. If we are unable to resolve your concern directly, we will support you to contact the appropriate external bodies, including the NDIS Quality and Safeguards Commission, if required.

Your Feedback Helps Strengthen Support

Thank you for taking the time to share your thoughts — we genuinely appreciate your input and use it to make NLA better for everyone we support.
