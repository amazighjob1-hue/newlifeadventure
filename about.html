About Us

Who We Are

New Life Adventure Disability (NLA) is a registered NDIS provider based in Kellyville Ridge, NSW. We support people with disability and their families across Western Sydney and surrounding regions.
Our work is guided by one simple belief: every person deserves support that respects their choices, protects their dignity, and helps them live a fulfilling life.
We work with children, young people, and adults with a wide range of support needs, including physical disabilities, intellectual disabilities, psychosocial disabilities, and complex care requirements.

Our Mission

To empower people with disability to live meaningful and independent lives by providing the right tools, services, and support in a way that protects choice and control.

Our Vision

To be a trusted and reliable NDIS provider known for culturally inclusive care, strong relationships, and support that genuinely makes a difference in people’s lives.

Our Values

At New Life Adventure Disability, Support That Puts You First is more than a slogan — it guides everything we do.

We provide personalised, reliable, and culturally inclusive support that is shaped around you as a whole person, not just your NDIS plan. Our focus is on building trust, understanding your needs, and delivering support that genuinely improves your day-to-day life.

Respect

We treat every participant with dignity and understanding.

Integrity

We communicate honestly and act with transparency and accountability.

Inclusivity

We welcome people from all cultural backgrounds, languages, faiths, and communities.

Excellence

We are committed to continuous improvement and high standards of care.

Our Team

Our team includes experienced support workers, coordinators, nurses, and allied health professionals who are passionate about making a positive difference.
We are proud to have a multicultural team that reflects the diversity of the communities we serve. Where possible, we match participants with workers who understand their language and cultural background.
All team members are appropriately qualified, screened, and trained in line with NDIS Quality and Safeguards Commission standards.

Our Commitment to Quality and Safety

As a registered NDIS provider, we operate in accordance with NDIS Quality and Safeguards Commission requirements. We maintain appropriate insurances, clear policies and procedures, and structured systems to ensure safe and reliable service delivery.
We actively seek feedback from participants and families to continuously improve our services.
At NLA, we do not see participants as numbers we see individuals with goals, strengths, and potential.
We are here to walk alongside you on your journey.
