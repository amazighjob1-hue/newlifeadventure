Supported Independent Living (SIL)

Supported Independent Living (SIL) services help participants live as independently as possible while receiving the support they need in a safe and comfortable environment.

At New Life Adventure Disability (NLA), we work closely with participants, families and allied health professionals to create living arrangements that support independence while ensuring the right level of assistance.

Accommodation Support

🏠

Housing Options

We assist participants in identifying suitable housing options that match their support needs and personal preferences.

🔧

Home Modifications

Where required, we help coordinate home modifications to improve accessibility, safety and comfort.

🧑‍⚕️

Allied Health Collaboration

We work with occupational therapists and healthcare professionals to ensure homes meet accessibility and mobility requirements.

Daily Living Support

🧹

Household Tasks

Support with cleaning, cooking and maintaining a safe and comfortable living environment.

📅

Daily Organisation

Assistance managing daily routines and maintaining structured living habits.

💰

Budgeting & Life Skills

Developing practical skills such as budgeting, meal planning and managing everyday responsibilities.

🌱

Building Independence

Encouraging participants to develop confidence and independence in their daily lives.

24/7 Support

For participants who require a higher level of care, NLA can coordinate round-the-clock support through our experienced support workers and trusted healthcare partners.

Our goal is to ensure participants receive continuous care, safety and support while maintaining independence within their home environment.

How SIL Works at NLA

1

Initial Assessment

We work with participants and allied health professionals to understand individual needs and goals.

2

Personalised Planning

A tailored support plan is developed to encourage independence while ensuring appropriate care.

3

Daily Support

Our support workers provide practical assistance and skill-building in everyday life.

4

Ongoing Review

Support plans are regularly reviewed to ensure participants continue progressing toward independence.
