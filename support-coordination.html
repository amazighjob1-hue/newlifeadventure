Helping You Navigate Your NDIS Journey

Navigating the NDIS can sometimes feel overwhelming. At NLA, our Support Coordination services are designed to help participants understand their plans, connect with the right providers and make the most of their supports.
Our coordinators work closely with participants, families and service providers to ensure that supports are delivered smoothly and aligned with each person’s goals.

Support Coordination

Our Support Coordinators help participants:

📄

Understand and implement your NDIS plan

🤝

Connect with trusted providers

⚙️

Coordinate supports across services

🚀

Build confidence to manage supports

We focus on empowering participants so they can gradually take greater control of their supports and decisions.

Get Support Coordination

Plan Management

NLA also assists participants with managing the financial side of their NDIS plan.
This includes:

💳

Process invoices and payments

📊

Track funding and budgets

📑

Provide clear financial reports

💡

Help you understand your funding

Our goal is to reduce the administrative burden so participants can focus on achieving their goals.
